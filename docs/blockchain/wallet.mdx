---
title: "Wallet"
---
The StateMesh Wallet is the official wallet for the StateMesh network. Besides performing basic tasks like transfers and viewing your account activity, it allows users and validators to manage features like staking, delegation and new validator registration.

The wallet is available online at:

- For testnet: [https://wallet-test.statemesh.org](https://wallet-test.statemesh.org)
- For mainnet: [https://wallet.statemesh.org](https://wallet.statemesh.org)
